# 12 — Automations

## Email & CRM automations
- Abandoned form reminders
- Welcome flows для новых порталов/сотрудников
- Follow-up after appointment (email with care instructions)

## Интеграции
- Вставляемая форма на сайт (embed) → HTTP webhook → Leads in CRM
- Instagram webhook → mentions/messages → create lead
- Google Calendar sync for appointments

## Marketing automations
- Auto post of new blog articles to social (AI generated captions)
- Campaigns for reactivation (clients without visit > X months)
